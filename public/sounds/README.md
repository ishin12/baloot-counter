# Custom Arabic callouts

Record these as MP3 files with the exact filenames. Missing files automatically use the browser's Arabic voice.

| Filename | Trigger | Phrase |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `roundWin.mp3` | Your team wins the round | كفو يا وحوش… كذا القيد ولا بلاش! |
| `roundLose.mp3` | Your team loses the round | حتى الحسبة ما وقفت معكم! |
| `buyerFell.mp3` | The buying team falls | راحت الطلبة يا حبيبي! |
| `leadBig.mp3` | Your lead first reaches 40+ | يا ساتر، وينهم؟ توّهم يدورون النشرة! |
| `trailBig.mp3` | You first trail by 40+ | شدّوا حيلكم… النشرة صارت طويلة عليكم! |
| `gameWin.mp3` | Your team wins the match | صبّوا القهوة… المجلس مجلسنا الليلة! |
| `gameLose.mp3` | Your team loses the match | قهوة للغالب… والخاسر يغسل الفناجيل! |
| `invalid.mp3` | Invalid entry | الحسبة فيها علوم… راجعها! |

Recording direction: a confident Saudi voice, close microphone, dry room, playful rather than insulting, and under three seconds per line.
